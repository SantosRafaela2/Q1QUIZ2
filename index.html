The Nightmare Before Christmas

The Nightmare Before Christmas is a stop motion film by Tim Burton and Micheal McDowell. In this film, there are towns or worlds for each holiday. Theres one for Easter, Thanksgiving, Christmas, and Halloween. Jack Skellington is the pumkin king of Halloween Town who plans all of the horrors and terrors for when their time comes on Halloween. Until Jack discovers.. Christmas

Jack Skellington - The protaginist and the Pumkin King in charge of Halloween Town who gets interested in the concept of Christmas

Sally Finklestein - A rag doll monster who tries her best to get out of the control of the doctor who created her. She also accompanies Jack in his journey through out the film.

Oogie Boogie - The antagonist and also known as the Boogyman. He is a bug-filled sack with a knack for gambling and cannibalism.
